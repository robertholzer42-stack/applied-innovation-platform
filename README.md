# Resilient Innovation Agent Platform

An AI-powered multi-dimensional innovation practice built on Braden Kelley's Resilient Innovation framework. Uses 13 specialized agents that systematically apply Future Thinking, Design Thinking, and Systems Thinking together to produce innovations that are desirable, viable, and resilient across multiple futures.

## What This Is

Most organizations innovate through a single lens. They run design thinking workshops but ignore the systems their ideas have to survive in. They do scenario planning but never test whether anyone wants what they're building. One-dimensional innovation fails.

This platform fixes that. It orchestrates 13 AI agents, each with a distinct personality, analytical framework, and output format, into a repeatable engagement pipeline that covers:
- **Product & service innovation**
- **Business model innovation**
- **Process & operational innovation**
- **Strategic foresight & disruption preparedness**

## Agent Architecture

### Tier 1: Client Interface
| Agent | Codename | Role |
|-------|----------|------|
| Client Intake | **Navigator** | Challenge discovery, maturity assessment, engagement scoping |
| Deliverables | **Publisher** | Board-ready decks, reports, workshop guides, case studies |

### Tier 2: Core Thinking
| Agent | Codename | Framework |
|-------|----------|-----------|
| Future Thinking | **Scout** | IFTF-aligned Prepare-Foresight-Insight cycle, tiered toolkit |
| Design Thinking | **Empathy** | Human-centered design: empathize, define, ideate, prototype |
| Systems Thinking | **Architect** | Draw Out Consequences, causal loops, leverage points |

### Tier 3: Intersection
| Agent | Codename | Role |
|-------|----------|------|
| Future + Design | **Visionary** | Future personas, speculative design, scenario prototyping |
| Design + Systems | **Integrator** | Scalable solutions, pilot-to-production, adoption pathways |
| Future + Systems | **Sentinel** | Resilience mapping, stress testing, adaptive strategy |

### Tier 4: Operational
| Agent | Codename | Role |
|-------|----------|------|
| Competitive Intel | **Radar** | Market monitoring, patent scanning, positioning analysis |
| Portfolio Mgmt | **Banker** | Horizon 1/2/3 balancing, pipeline health, resource allocation |
| Metrics & Scoring | **Scorekeeper** | DVFA scoring, innovation maturity assessment, KPI design |
| Change Mgmt | **Bridge** | Adoption readiness, stakeholder resistance, transition design |

### Tier 5: Orchestration
| Agent | Codename | Role |
|-------|----------|------|
| Orchestrator | **Conductor** | Cross-agent synthesis, conflict resolution, unified strategy |
